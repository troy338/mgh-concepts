About Clever Kitchens

A local maker you can actually talk to

Clever Kitchens is a Seaford-based custom cabinet maker. We design, manufacture and install kitchens and cabinetry across Melbourne's south-east and the Mornington Peninsula — everything under one roof, managed by Grant and the team.

We built our reputation the slow way: quality work, treating people's homes with respect, and referrals from clients who love what we made for them.
